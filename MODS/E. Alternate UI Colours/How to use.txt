1. Using notepad++ (https://notepad-plus-plus.org/downloads/), find the WTCS5 folder, open the settings folder, and open tcs settings.xml

2. Find the colour scheme you want to use, Dark Mode or WTS Legacy Edition, at the of the list of the line which has "<colour name="bg negative"..." you'll find an -->. 

3. Select the arrow, and cut, and paste it next to the word "MODE" or edition. 

4. You have now made this active. Go in game and open the preferences with Shift+P.

5. In the bottom left of the preferences, click on the reset button, and press clear cache, then press Reload Skin.
